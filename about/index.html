👋 About me¶

Hi there, I’m Daniel, a data scientist based in Singapore.

I am currently working @Apple where I focus on experimentation for Apple Media Products. Previously, I worked @OCBC AI Labs on AI applications for banking use case and @TripAdvisor where I focused on the optimisation of the metasearch auction.

In my spare time, I enjoy teaching and have taught the Introduction to Data Science course at @General Assembly and @Vertical Institute.
